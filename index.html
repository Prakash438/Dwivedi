About

Mr. Suresh Prasad Dwivedi of DwivediDairyFarm

Mr. Suresh Prasad Dwivedi. comes from many genrations of dairy farmers and has been influenced by his elders to do good. He himself started off by collecting milk from the nearby villages and selling it in the market. He then expanded himself by investing more into the business, bought cattle. And today DwivedidairyFarms is a fully fledged business with a variety of livestock. Now, DwivediDairyFarms not only deals with Milk, but also various dairy products ranging from desi ghee to cheese and curd. Mr. Suresh has always seen satisfying the customer need to the fullest as his first priority.
